About Tote

Tote is a neighborhood-focused directory designed for discovery, built for real people who live in or are exploring the vibrant neighborhoods of New York City.

In a world dominated by endless maps, confusing interfaces, and algorithmic suggestions, we believe in the power of a simple, scannable list. Finding a great cafe, a trusted local shop, or a neighborhood gem shouldn't require panning and zooming across a cluttered map.

Our goal is straightforward: provide curated, fast, and accessible lists of local businesses. No fluff, no distractions. Just the information you need, easily sortable and readable, so you can spend less time searching and more time experiencing the neighborhood.

Explore your local area. Support local businesses. Find it on Tote.
